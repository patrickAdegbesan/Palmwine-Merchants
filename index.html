About Us

Palmwine Merchants Co is an Afrocentric lifestyle and entertainment brand that celebrates palmwine, bushmeat and open-flame cuisine in creative and modern ways. The brand is rooted in African tradition but styled for a modern audience.

We seek to elevate palmwine beyond the local gourd or calabash into stylish presentations — bottles, calabashes, cocktails, and custom containers.

For B2B & Hospitality

Looking to stock palmwine or host a themed activation? We supply restaurants, lounges, hotels, and event planners with premium palmwine formats and experiential service.
